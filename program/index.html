Workshop Program: Day 1

9:00am - 9:30am Welcome & opening ceremony
Speakers, workshop organizers and participants introduction.
9:30am - 5:00pm Introduction to R
Jacqueliene Materu, Leacky Muchene, Sophia Kagoye, Neema Mosha, Wende Safari
Topics to be covered include: introduction to the R environment, data wrangling, visualization with ggplot and reproducible research using RMarkdown with version control (GIT).

Workshop Program: Day 2

9:00am - 5:00pm Gaussian outcomes (Normal distribution)
Jacqueliene Materu, Innocent Mboya, Leacky Muchene, Neema Mosha, Wende Safari
Refresher on linear regression, r functions and loops, introduction to simulations all with adequate time dedicated to a hands-on practical session where participants will work in small groups on a dedicated dataset.

Workshop Program: Day 3

9:00am - 5:00pm Binary outcomes (binomial distribution)
Jacqueliene Materu, Innocent Mboya, Leacky Muchene, Wende Safari
Refresher on logistic regression and generalized linear mixed models, an introduction to machine learning (classification, crossvalidation), all with adequate time dedicated to a hands-on practical session where participants will work in small groups on a dedicated dataset.

Workshop Program: Day 4

9:00am - 5:00pm Poisson regression and Survival analysis
Jim Todd, Jacqueliene Materu, Leacky Muchene, Neema Mosha, Wende Safari
Refresher on Poisson regression and an introduction to survival analysis. Non-linear model fitting in R with a focus on optimizing user defined objective functions, all interpersed with adequate time for hands-on practical sessions.

Workshop Program: Day 5

9:00am - 5:00pm Recap on learning objectives and introduction to future topics
Leacky Muchene
A high-level introduction to developing Shiny applications in R. Subsequently, presenters will make group presentations on the projects worked on during the week followed by a questions and answers session.
Closing ceremony
